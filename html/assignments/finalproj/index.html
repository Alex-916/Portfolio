AfterShock 2016

The 2016 Monster Energy Aftershock Festival had a lineup that was certain to generate some seismic activity in the area. The two-day music festival, now in its fifth year, was set to return to Sacramento's Discovery Park the weekend of Oct. 22 and 23 with headliners Tool and Avenged Sevenfold leading the way. Aftershock 2017

California’s biggest rock festival, Monster Energy’s Aftershock roars into Sacramento’s Discovery Park October 21-22.
